 WELCOME TO G.H RAISONI COLLEGE

G H Raisoni College of Engineering and Management, Pune was established in the year 2006. G H Raisoni College has a material designed campus spread over 11 acres of lush green environment in the posh location of Greater Kailash. The Campus comprises of 4 different blocks which are surrounded by heavy green gardens and lakes and neatly designed paths to connect the blocks with each other.

G H Raisoni College offers Under Graduate and Post Graduate level full time Professional programs approved by AICTE in affiliation with SPPU UNIVERSITY, Pune.
